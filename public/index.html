Unifyze is a boutique search firm specializing in securing top talent for our clients in the San Francisco Bay Area as well as Southern California and other major markets. We are comprised of a group of veterans in the tech recruiting industry that possesses the skills to get the job done in this competitive industry. We specialize in full time permanent positions and believe in making the commitment between company and candidate long-lasting.

We unify companies and candidates in a new process called Unifyze. To unify is defined as "to make into a unit or a coherent whole." Our team takes pride in growing the relationship side of the business not only with leading companies who employ us to find candidates but also with professionals who are looking for their ideal position as well. Our dual-sided commitment breeds true unity. When the perfect company and the ideal candidate are introduced, they truly become a cohesive unity - hence our process - Unifyze.

Here at Unifyze, we approach every search as a team. The well-connected professionals who make up Unifyze each boast their own networks, unique style, varied personalities, and bring talent to the table that collectively ensures success for those we are chartered to match. As the client or candidate, you will not have just one recruiter working hard for you. The whole Unifyze team will have your back in your search. This collaborative atmosphere results in a great workplace atmosphere that we sincerely hope transfers to the professional relationship between candidates and our organizational clients.

We welcome the challenge of finding candidates at all levels (Executive level, new graduate, lead, senior level) for placement in the Software Engineering arena. Whether you are a two person stealth mode start-up or maybe working in a division of a Fortune 500 company, Unifyze can help. The tech industry is a huge one but revolves around a very "small world" where connections and relationships go a long way!

Let Unifyze utilize the expansive networks we've created through hard work over the years to introduce you to your next employee or employer.
